Future updates

We are committed to help you breakthrough the Intermediate English Plateau.

Vocabulary builder

Utilizing the Spaced Repetition AI, it will help you memorize relevant words you've saved. You can also ask the AI on how to pronounce the words and how to use the them in context.

IELTS Writing trainer

Using Generative text and image AI, it will provide you an IELTS-style writing test question you can answer to. It will provide feedback, correction, and alternative answer to that question.

IELTS Speaking trainer

Using the same Generative AI technolgy, it will ask you an IELTS-style speaking test question. You can go with Easy Mode, in which you'll be presented with the answer you can read aloud and let the AI assess your pronunciation etc.

Conversational exercise

If you prefer a more casual approach, you can invite our Conversational AI to discuss any topic. It will ask you a more casual questions while also giving you constructive and actional grammar feedback for every given answer.
